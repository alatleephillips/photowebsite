Alex Phillips

I started taking pictures in high school with film cameras from thrift stores and my dad's well-loved Pentax lenses. Over the years as I pursued my career as a scientist I turned to photography as a creative escape. During my PhD, I eagerly grew my film collection and invested in a digital camera. Now, I shoot individuals, couples, and famillies. Hit me up and let's make some magic - on film or digital.

I'm based in Santa Barbara, but I'm down to travel and don't charge extra fees for photoshoots within Southern California.
